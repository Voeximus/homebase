5 · When to put the money in

Two sweeps a month — right after each check clears its bills.

Your cash lands on paydays (~15th & ~29th); rent leaves on the 1st. So don't attack debt at the start of the month when cash is committed to rent — sweep the leftover to the target debt right after each paycheck, once that cycle's bills are covered.
